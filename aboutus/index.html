关于我们 || About us

关于我们 || About us

亚欧联合组织是一个经验丰富的团队，我们致力于创建一个科技和商业相结合的专业团队。让亚洲和欧洲连接的更加紧密。

Asia-Euro Synchronization is an initiative created by a group of experienced technical and business professionals to connect Asia and Europe closer together.

在亚洲和欧洲的学术商业环境中，我们拥有深度而立体宽广的知识。我们的目标就是在学术和商业中建立一个技术的桥梁。
通过我们的努力，将高等院校，公司，各种组织间的交流联结起来。

With our diverse and wide set of knowledge about both the Asian and European academic and business environment, our aim is to form a technological transfer bridge between all participants. Through our activities, we strive to create a better technological future by enabling closer collaboration and knowledge exchange between universities, companies, supporting organizations and students from Asia and Europe.

我们相信，以下三个方面是我们共同发展的基石：合作教学，合作研究和集群合作。

We believe that three elements are required for a closer common development: Educational programs, research partnerships and cluster collaborations.
​
我们的合作教学项目是由我们的合作精英大学所主办的。学员可以到欧洲，近距离了解当地的工业环境，同时可以增强他们的专业技能。为今后的职业发展，和持续学习起到至关重要的作用和影响。

Our educational programs are executed in partnership with leading universities, allowing Asian students and professionals to travel to Europe. Participants gain a comprehensive insight into the local industrial situation while at the same time enhancing their professional skills and career prospects through additional education.

合作研究项目，工业产业和研究院可以深入合作。可以加速研究成果的转化。同时，让亚欧的公司可以结合各自的独有的能力创造更好的未来。

Research partnerships are enabled by connecting industrial and research institutes through common events. This broadens the scope and speed of technology transfer between all parties, allowing European and Asian companies to take advantage of each other’s unique abilities and features.

集群合作讲通过我们的合作网络来激励我们从国家之间合作到超越国际间合作的进化。

Cluster collaborations are encouraged on a national and supra-national level by leveraging our existing network of partners.

通过结合这个三个元素。我们相信我们有更好的未来，也将为世界做出自己的贡献

By combining all three elements, we believe that we can contribute to a stronger and better world.

通常的问题 || Common program questions

我申请项目的时候是否会收到一封邀请函来帮助我获得签证？
Will participants receive an invitation letter to obtain their VISA?
是的。 我们会寄送邀请函帮助你成功申请签证。
Yes. A program invitation letter will be provided for successful applicants.

课程结构是怎样的？
How are lectures conducted?
课程是由我们拥有丰富经验的教授和业界具有丰富实践经验的工程师们对各位学员进行教学。教学地点是在我们的欧洲公司和合作工厂。
非常注重教育的互动和实践性。
Lectures are conducted at our European facilities by experienced professors and industry practitioners. Lectures are highly interactive and focused on maximum practical benefit.

哪些亚洲学校和结构可以派遣学生来参加我们的项目？
Which Asia universities or institutes are eligible to send students?

我们挑选参与的学校和机构是基于他们的简历和社会工作情况。如果你是一名学生或希望短期学习的工程师，请向你的教授或主管提出申请，我们可以代为沟通。
We select participants based on merit as shown in their CV. Partnering universities’ students will have an advantage if the merit based review seems inconclusive. If you are interested in having your university partner with us, let us know and we talk with your dean or professor.

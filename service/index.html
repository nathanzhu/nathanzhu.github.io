共建研究实验室 || Research Labs Establishment

我们很荣幸的宣布，亚欧联合诅咒首先在印度和欧洲同步组织了一个研究实验室，支持着亚洲和欧洲之间的合作伙伴间的交流合作

It is our pleasure to announce that Asia-Euro Synchronization took a giant step to organize the establishment of research labs in India to support the Indian SME’s with the cooperation of European Research Partners.

我们现在的领域主要是工业自动化和机器人。我们也提供仿真软件和具备多种技术设备的实验室设施。同时，我们开始建立了远程自动化实验室。在亚洲的公司将会支持德国亚琛的欧洲机电一体化中心APS GmbH。

​We are initiating this in the field of Industrial Automation and Robotics. As a initial step of the current mission we are starting with establishment of Remote Automation labs at Indian university by providing the simulation software support and technology and research lab facilities support from German execution partner “European Centre for Mechatronics – APS GmbH”, located in Aachen, Germany.

先进的工业机器人控制实验室 || ARC - Advanced Robotic Control Lab for Industrial Applications

世界各地的大学都在不断提高更新自己的教育理念。为了学生们可以有充实的实践经验。让学生可以更加好的适应商务生活。

Universities worldwide improve their educational concepts continuously. However, they lack concrete training for job with hands-on experiences, so that the students are fit for the business life.

亚欧同步组织组织建立研发实验室，率先在印度和德国亚琛的欧洲机电一体化中心的APS GmbH展开。还有德国的‘Steinbeis Center for Technology Transfer’提供技术支持。这样做的目的是教机器人在亚洲的大学，与工业产业中的机器人公司可以有更广泛的合作。让教育理念更新。让亚洲的工业，包括中小企业提供的真实的生产要求进行测试的自动化解决方案。另外，也间接参与全球研究计划的未来。

Asia Euro Synchronization tackles this problem and organizes the establishment of R&D labs in India, with the worldwide renowned partners ‘APS GmbH – European Centre for Mechatronics’, Germany and ‘Steinbeis Center for Technology Transfer’, India. The aim is to enhance the current education by introducing concrete industrial automation concepts by teaching robotics at Asia universities, with access to real industrial robot. Further, these labs can support Asia industries including SMEs by providing automation solutions tested under real production requirements. In addition, the gap is bridged to participate at global research programs in the future.

下发是率先开展的印度大学条件，其他地方请发邮件详细咨询。

An initial step for the implementation, ARC had already been organizing regular student seminars in Germany including workshops and live experiments at APS in Germany. ARC provides the students the access to industrial robotics under production conditions with remote control. An extensive exchange program for Indian faculty members guarantees the high quality of the educational lessons and practical courses. These practical courses allow direct control of the industrial robots in Germany from Indian universities. Concretely, the students learn simulating, programming and controlling in order to test their results at these industrial robots, which are equipped with grippers, sensors etc.

Applied Robotics Control for Industrial Automation:
Each participating university receives a software access for simulation and control of industrial robots.
Students learn individually and as a team to solve concrete industrial tasks which are splits into various suitable sub-tasks for immediate learning success. These tasks are supervised by trained Indian faculty members along with remotely participating German experts.
The automation tasks are designed and developed in cooperation with the Indian faculty members.
​
Requirement for Indian University:
Indian University/College need be a member of Indo-Euro Synchronization academic cluster. For further inquiries on obtaining the membership please contact us at consult@indoeurosync.com

Benefits of the Membership and Remote robotic lab at Indian University:
Member of our Academic cluster will receive the necessary training for their faculty members at Germany to run this program
Software and its server support will be provided to members of the universities to establish automation lab facilities at their location
These universities are the premium members of our near future Automation Cluster in India to execute the industrial and research projects in next days.
Trained students will be the first point of contact for internship positions and jobs in our projects and partner automation industries from India and Germany.

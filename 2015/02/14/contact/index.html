咨询时间 || Hours and information

随时为你服务

We are available.

在亚欧联合组织，我们乐意与你们交流。我们喜欢随时回答你们的问题。

At Asia Euro Synchronization, we like to communicate quickly. Send us a note or email and we will answer within 24 hours (sometimes even on weekends). And if it’s urgent, you can call us on our phone.

告诉我们你的经验

Tell us about your experience.

我们希望听到你对参加我们的活动有什么好的帮助和建议。

请发邮件：blueshuchen@gmail.com

We want to hear how your courses are progressing and how attending Asia Euro Synchronization has impacted your life. Is there something we can do better, or did we get something just right? Let us know.

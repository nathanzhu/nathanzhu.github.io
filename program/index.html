AutoSol 2015

这是一个短期的游学项目。 为亚洲的学生和工程师搭建一座桥梁。联通和欧洲中心的技术和学术工业领域。
​
This an initiative taken up by AsiaEuroSync, by understanding the actual importance of upgrading technological knowledge of Asia students/engineers and for a better technological future, we form a technology transfer bridge between Indian and European; academic and industry segments.

AutoSol，是一个难得机会，让学生和工程师能够有机会参与欧洲尖端的机械科学技术。ECE，EEE，CSE，”汽车机电一体化”。

AutoSol, will be the unique opportunity for students to obtain cutting edge European technology in the fields of Mechanical, ECE, EEE, CSE, Automotive & Mechatronics. AutoSol would be promising its participants unprecedented outlook into the technology world.

这一项目由四个方面组成：

This program mainly consisting 4 modules:

为期两天的研讨会：欧洲机电一体化研究室，在亚琛，AutoSol为未来的工程师提供了为了技术研讨会。参与研讨会的有德国知名的学者和专家。
同时，在会上，学生和工程师可以提交他们的论文。最好的三篇将会登载在我们的杂志上。

Two days of seminars at ‘European Labs for Mechatronics’, Aachen: AutoSol also offers future oriented seminars for prospective engineers. Two day seminars are being offered by renowned German professors and industrial experts for an in-depth information of the present and future market conditions. These seminars would be taking place at the ‘European labs for Mechatronics’.
Student Conference: With this opportunity students can present their paper’s on various technological sectors related to Industrial Applications. Best three papers will be awarded and these papers will be published in our journal.

在汉诺威的交易研讨会，AutoSol提供了一个极好的机会，满足并与来自各个领域的业者。汽车制造商和他们的代表将在汉诺威工业展上交流体会，学生将参与国际就业市场。这种额外的好处是顶尖亚洲公司可以参与到国际交流中来。

Trade Fair & Workshops at Hannover Messe 2015: AutoSol offers an explicit opportunity to meet and interact with top companies from various fields, automotive manufacturers and their representatives in their respective outlets at Hannover Messe 2015. It followed with Career and Job fair, where student will expose to the International Job market. This additional advantage would be interacting top Asia companies as Asia as a partner country for this fair.

大众工厂之旅，参与德国最大的大众造车厂。

理解实际的造车技术。提供各种技术参考。

Volkswagen Manufacturing Plant Trip: There is also a one day Industrial trip to Volkswagen Manufacturing Pant in order to provide the participants with a brief idea of the biggest car manufacturing plant in Germany.
With these activities we are trying to provide the Research, Academic, Business- Career opportunities along with Industrial trip to manufacturing plant of Volkswagen

项目亮点：杰出的参与者将会有机会参加德国的4-6个实习项目。参与欧洲的机电一体化项目。

Program Highlights: Outstanding candidates go to Germany for our 4-6 Internship program at “European Center for Mechatronics, Aachen, Germany”

我们希望这一项目给你提供好的帮助，收到你们良好的反应。

We hope that, we will receive good response on this program from your students and organization. We will be glad to hear that.

SynergyRules 是一所杰出的综合性民营公司。专注于机械自动化的外包设计和解决方案。SynergyRules的核心支柱是解决速度，专业服务，良好客户沟通。在机械自动化的突破性技术领域中，已经有了很多成功的项目。现在，SynergyRules正在努力实习一流的控制系统和系统过程自动化技术。采用了卓越的IT技术和快速响应的电流技术需求。SynergyRules和其合作伙伴拥有丰富的经验。拥有各类专家为客户处理复杂的程序。

SynergyRules is an eminent integrated private company focused on process automation, outsourcing and outtasking solutions. SynergyRules believes in speed, professionalism and client communication as the core pillars of a successful business. The company has had ample experience in successfully managing Indo-European projects in the fields of process automation techniques and ground breaking technology solutions. SynergyRules strives to meet the current demands of companies trying to achieve process automation techniques with best in class control system systems, using a superior IT focus to enable system integrations per agile practices. SynergyRules and its partners have vast experience and are experts in handling numerous complex programs for their clients.

SCTI 是Technology Transfer Network Centre of Steinbeis Foundation, 技术传播网络中心。用于德国和印度海德拉巴两间办公室。连接了850多个中心，不仅在欧洲，也是在其他国家，如中国，俄罗斯和新加坡的全球网络的一部分。终于专注于技术传播，并且拥有良好的资源（实验室和专家资源），因此，中心对于客户的业务可以提供专业的评估，对其全局进行进一步支持。中西还可以提供合同和项目管理方面的专业知识。

SCTI is a Technology Transfer Network Centre of Steinbeis Foundation, Germany, with its office at Hyderabad, India. It is a part of Global network connecting more than 850 Centres, not just in Europe, but also in other countries like China, Russia and Singapore. Each Centre specializes in its own stream of technology and has excellent resources in terms of laboratories and expert faculty. Hence the industry interested to outsource its technology development work with an institution does it at a fraction of cost involved – that if it were to borne the entire cost of its own. This is further backed by enormous knowledge resources, which can be assessed by each Centre by networking with its peer institutions within the Steinbeis network globally. The STCI provides its expertise in terms of contract and project management so as to provide the services in the most professional manner.

APS GmbH，成立于1981年，总部设在亚琛，是欧洲的机电一体化中心。 APS有超过30年的机器人，传感器，信息和通信技术领域的开发经验。我们永远想要的经验丰富的技术工程师和跨学科的综合方法技术。微末的潜在客户，国家和国际工业合作伙伴，政府机构，研究院提供出色的解决方案。特别值得aps特别关注小型和中型的企业的创新技术引进和使用。通过在研发领域取得的丰富经验，APS在其中起着至关重要的作用。

APS GmbH, headquartered in Aachen was founded in 1981 and has been awarded as the European Centre for Mechatronics. APS has already more than 30 years of experience in the area of robotics, sensor technology, information and communication technology. Developing these technologies with experienced engineers and technicians from various disciplines with multidisciplinary approaches, these solutions are provided to the potential clients in cooperation with national and international industrial partners, public authorities and researchers. A particular concern is to support small and medium-sized enterprises in the introduction and use of innovative technologies. By gaining vast experience in the field of research and development, APS plays a vital role in providing the top in seminars in recently advanced technologies.
